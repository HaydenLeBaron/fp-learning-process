---
title: I.Todo
---

Used to "tag" notes as being members of a set of notes under construction or in need of editing/revision.

## Properties
- $n \rightarrow I \implies n \text{ has an internal task}$

## Usage

Ex:
```md

---

This is an example note

- [[I.Atomic]]

- [[I.Todo]] : Read through and link to
```
